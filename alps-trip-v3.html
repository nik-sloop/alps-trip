Day 2 · Day Trip
Garmisch-Partenkirchen
Sunday, May 10
▾
🚂
Innsbruck → Garmisch-Partenkirchen 6:38am
Arrives 7:57am (~1hr 20min). Early start is non-negotiable.
🚞
Cogwheel Train (RB64) → Eibsee
8:15am or 9:15am, 45min. €2.10 one-way or €7 day pass — buy the day pass.
🥾
Lake Eibsee Loop — 4.5 miles
Start on the right side. Turquoise lake with Zugspitze as backdrop. Flat and easy.
🏔️
Zugspitze Cable Car Book Early
€69/pp. Germany's highest peak. Tickets may only open 30 days out — watch from April 10.
🍽️
Panorama Lounge 2962
Lunch at the Zugspitze summit. Altitude dining at 9,718ft.
Afternoon
🚞
Cogwheel → Garmisch, Bus to Partenkirchen
2:15pm train → Garmisch. 3:02pm bus 321/322 → Partenkirchen (10min).
🥾
Partnachklamm Gorge €10/pp
30min one way. Dramatic narrow gorge — a completely different experience from the lake.
🚂
Return: Garmisch → Innsbruck (RB6)
6:02pm–7:23pm. Back for dinner in Innsbruck.
💡
Backup: Mount Wank Cable Car Alt.
€30, confirmed open. 1.4mi loop. If Zugspitze sells out or weather closes the peak.
Day 3 · Innsbruck + Transfer
Nordkette → Bolzano
Monday, May 11
▾
🎟️
Nordkette 3 Cable Cars Booked
$208 total (4 tickets). Ascend 8–9:30am time slot. Top of Innsbruck, high alpine terrain.

Breakfast: Cloud 9 / Seegrube
Coffee and pastries at mid-station with panoramic Alpine views. Start the day right.

Nordkette Hike — ~3 miles

Riverside lunch in Old Town before departing south.
🚂
Innsbruck → Bolzano Skip Eurorail
S3 to Brenner (€11/pp) then Bolzano Card covers the rest. ~2hr 10min, runs every 30min.
🍽️
Dinner in Bolzano
South Tyrolean cuisine — halfway between Italian and Austrian. Explore the old piazzas.
🏨
Hotel Stiegl Scala Booked
Nights 3–4 · 2 rooms · $791. Bolzano Card included. Luggage storage after checkout.
Day 4 · Dolomites
Val Gardena Day Trip
Tuesday, May 12
▾
🚌
Bus 350: Bolzano → Ortisei 7:26am
1hr. Early departure maximises cable car time. Or train + bus transfer.
🚡
Seiser Alm Cable Car €35 RT
8:30am–5pm. Europe's largest high-altitude meadow. Confirm May schedule 1 week before.
🥾
Monte Piz – Malga Schgaguler Loop — 4.8 miles
Go counter-clockwise. Sweeping Dolomite views throughout. Best hike of the Bolzano stay.
💡
Alt if lifts closed: St. Jakob Church Hike — 3.5mi
No lifts needed. Always accessible. Forest and meadow trail to a beautiful hilltop church.
🥾
Alt: Mount Piz Hike If time
If arriving Ortisei by 2:30 — explore some higher alpine terrain.
🥾
Backup: Oswald Promenade
Easy walk in Bolzano town alongside Guncina if weather is bad.
🚌
Bus 350: Ortisei → Bolzano
Leaves every hour from 2:59pm. 1hr back.
💡
Optional Add-on: Castelrotto + Marinzen Cable Car Alt.
Bus 172 Ortisei → Castelrotto. Marinzen cable car €20 RT. Only if making great time.

🍽️
Dinner: Rino or L'ora Della Pasta
L'ora also works as a casual quick lunch the next morning. Both solid.

Day 8 · Interlaken
Oeschinen Lake + Mürren / Schilthorn
Saturday, May 16
▾
🚂
Interlaken → Kandersteg 6:29am start
1hr, 1 transfer. 15min walk to cable car. Very early — set two alarms.
🚡
Oeschinensee Cable Car Booked
144 CHF total (4 people). Ascend 8:30–10am. Emerald glacial lake ringed by waterfalls.
🥾
Oeschinen Lake Trails — C / D / G
C = easy lake loop; D = panorama; G = mountain. Check snow conditions that week.
🍽️
Mountain Restaurant at Oeschinen
Lakeside lunch at the top before heading down. Great rösti and views.
🚂
Kandersteg → Interlaken
11:14am or 12:14pm (1hr, every hour). Back for afternoon at Mürren.
Afternoon — Mürren & Schilthorn
🚂🚡
Interlaken → Lauterbrunnen → Mürren
Train (22min) + cable car + 11min train. Fully covered by Eurorail pass.
🏔️
Schilthorn Cable Car €68 w/ Eurorail
World's steepest cable car. Piz Gloria 360° restaurant at top. James Bond was filmed here.
🍽️
Piz Gloria Revolving Restaurant
Full 360° panorama while you eat — Jungfrau, Eiger, Mönch. Unique dining experience.
🥾
Thrill Walk at Birg — Glass Walk + Tightrope
Stop here on the way down. Last lift to Mürren at 5:50pm — don't miss it.
💡
Allmendhubel Cable Car, Mürren Alt. to Schilthorn
€16. Scenic viewpoint and restaurant. Good if group energy is low after the Oeschinen morning.
⛵
Lake Brienz Cruise Free w/ Eurorail
BLS cruise Interlaken → Brienz, 1hr 20min. 15:07 or 16:07 departure. Turquoise Alpine lake.
